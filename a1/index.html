GEOG 151 - World Regional Geography

Assignment One: Seeing Patterns

In this assignment, you will look at satellite data aggregated by Google Earth and describe the patterns that you see. You will look at ten randomly chosen images, and describe the patterns you see. Each description should be approximately 100 words (you can use the worksheet provided). Be sure you identify which location you're describing (shown in the lower right hand corner of the image)!

What should I say?

Focus on the transportation, settlement, and agricultural patterns that you see. Imagine that you're trying to explain to a friend who can't see it. Focus on color, pattern, texture, arrangement, and shape. Are things regular or irregular? Straight or curved? Triangular, rectangular, or circular? Don't focus on these alone: use your own words, and don't be afraid to use metaphors. What catches your eye? What do you think is going on? This sample may help you.

When you're ready, hit the random link button that will open a new window in your browser with a random image.

What do I turn in

A filled-in copy of the worksheet. Note that five are filled in, but ten are required.

Would you like to know more?
